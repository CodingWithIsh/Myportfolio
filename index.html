About




Hi I’m Ismail yusuf, I’m 22 and I am an aspiring software engineer.
I recently finished the tech talent accelerator course with Generation UK which is a 6 weeklong intensive programme designed to prepare and equip students with the mindsets and skill that employers are looking for.
This intensive 6-week course acts as a pre-apprenticeship course for Software Engineer Level 4 apprenticeships.

Prior to enrolling on this course, I was working as a plumbing apprentice for a
company based in east London. While this may seem like a drastic change in career
paths, a lot of the skills I learnt in plumbing are transferrable to software
engineering. For example, I was working with clients daily to determine their
needs, I was drawing diagrams to help the clients to envision how the work
would be carried out, I was collaborating with other plumbers and tradesmen
during big jobs and finally I was identifying faults in plumbing systems and
finding a solution which I have found to be very similar to debugging code.
